Privacy Policy

Updated April 3, 2026

Bandicoot is a word game made by Magic Factory. You can play without an account. We collect only what's needed to make the game work.

Data We Collect

Anonymous player ID
Created automatically via Supabase anonymous auth to save your game progress.
Email address
Only if you optionally link your account for cross-device sync. Used solely for authentication via magic link.
Display name
Shown on leaderboards. You choose what to display.
Game scores & stats
Submitted to the server for leaderboards, streaks, and personal statistics.
Game history
Words found, scores, mode played, and timestamps.

How We Use It

  • Save your progress and sync across devices
  • Display leaderboard rankings
  • Authenticate your account if you sign in
  • Local notifications are optional and processed entirely on-device

Third-Party Services

Supabase
Backend database and authentication. Privacy Policy
Sentry
Crash and error reporting. Captures device info, OS version, and error context — no personal data. Privacy Policy
Apple Game Center
Leaderboards and achievements. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

What We Don't Collect

  • No tracking or advertising
  • No data shared with third parties for marketing
  • No analytics beyond crash reporting
  • No cookies or cross-app tracking
  • No location data
  • No contacts or photos

Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days.

Security

Your data is stored securely via Supabase (US-hosted). All communication uses HTTPS encryption.

Your Rights

You can update or delete your profile info at any time. To request full account deletion, email support@magicfactory.dev.

Children

Bandicoot does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game can be played without providing any personal info.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

Questions? support@magicfactory.dev
